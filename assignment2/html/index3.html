LYNX POINT SIAMESE CATS

Lynx Point Siamese cats are gentle, vocal, and dog like!
Their breed inculdes a mixture of a Siamese cat and Tabby cat.
These cats typically live for 15-20 years!

Lynx Point Siamese cats have very unique patterns in their fur!
This breed was actually mistakingly created in the 1940's-1950's.
Although, this breed became popular around the 1960's. This
breed was given the name Lynx Point because they resemble a
wild Lynx!

Overall, If you are looking for a friendly, social, and beautiful
pet! These cats are just what you need! As long as you give them
love food, and clean their poo, you will have a long life friend to
keep you company!
